Card Stylization

There are four examples below with some sample configurations.

Style 1: This is the default for cards it uses the primary color as the backgrund color.

<div class="card1 s12 m6 l3">

Style 2: This adds larger rounding to the card but otherwise is the same a style 1.
<div class="card1 round s12 m6 l3">

Style 3: The clear tag on this makes the background transparent and adds a border around the card.
<div class="card1 clear s12 m6 l3">

Style 4: The clear tag on this makes the background transparent and adds a border around the card while adding larger rounding to the card. 
<div class="card1 round clear s12 m6 l3">
